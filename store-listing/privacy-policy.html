Muffin Voice is built by Thomas Arenberg. This policy explains what data the app handles, how it's used, and your rights. We believe in keeping things simple and transparent.

What Data Does the App Use?

Voice audio: When you have a conversation, your microphone audio is streamed in real-time to Google's Gemini API for processing. The audio is used solely to generate your AI companion's responses. We (the developer) never receive, store, or have access to your audio.

Camera and screen data: If you choose to share your camera or screen, those images are sent to Google's Gemini API during the active session for visual understanding. This is entirely optional and only happens when you explicitly enable it.

Conversation history: Summaries and transcripts of your conversations are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device.

Memory and preferences: The app remembers facts about you (like your name) to personalize conversations. This information is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and localStorage. It is not transmitted anywhere.

Usage data: The app tracks how many minutes you've used for the daily limit. This is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Muffin Voice uses Google's Gemini API to process voice audio and generate responses. When you have a conversation, your audio is sent to Google's servers. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's API Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services receive your data.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone. Ever.
  • We do not collect analytics or tracking data.
  • We do not store your conversations on any server.
  • We do not have access to your audio, camera, or screen data.

Local Storage Only

All app data ‚Äî conversation history, memory, preferences, and usage tracking ‚Äî is stored locally on your device using standard web storage technologies (IndexedDB and localStorage). You can clear this data at any time through the app's settings or by clearing your browser/app data.

Your Choices

  • Microphone: Required for voice conversations. You grant permission through your device's standard permission dialog.
  • Camera: Optional. Only activated when you tap the camera button.
  • Screen sharing: Optional. Only activated when you explicitly choose to share.
  • You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Muffin Voice is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
